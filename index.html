Sales Process - Step by Step

Complete visualization of the entire sales workflow

Complete Process Flow

1
Discovery Call
Schedule a 30min call with the prospect. Key details: Company size, budget, timeline, key pain points.
📱 Slack • 📧 Email • 📊 Template: discovery-call-checklist.pdf
2
Log Opportunity
Enter all discovery details into Salesforce. Create new Opportunity with budget estimate and expected close date.
🔧 Salesforce • 💡 Required fields: Amount, Close Date, Description
3
Create Proposal
Draft custom proposal tailored to prospect needs. Use template and modify pricing/scope based on requirements.
📝 Google Docs • 📌 Template: proposal-template.docx • ⏱️ ~45 min to complete
4
Send & Follow Up
Send proposal via email within 24hrs of discovery call. Schedule follow-up call for 48hrs later to discuss.
📧 Gmail • 📅 Calendar • 🔔 Set reminder for follow-up
